When maintenance of machinery (physical machines deployed on site) isn’t efficiently scheduled, equipment may need to be pulled off site for repairs at inconvenient and critical times.

Our Approach

Predictive maintenance of machinery equipment using an Intelligent Automation (ML and RPA) web-based application to predict signs of degradation, time of failure and involved physical components early by analyzing the telemetry data, collected by IoT sensors, fed into it in real time to efficiently plan maintenance schedules at convenient times minimizing the maintenance costs involved otherwise
